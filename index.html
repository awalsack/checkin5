About Me

Hello my name is Kitty Cat and I am a kitty cat, but sometimes I forget I am a kitty cat.

One day it was snowing really hard and I was really cold so I hid in a garage. I was hungry and cold and I wasn't feeling very well. Then, some nice people found me, wrapped me in a snuggly blanket, and took me into their home.

Now I am very fat and I live with my mommy in her apartment at Virginia Tech. I was not very happy with her when she put me in a car for a very long time to take me here, but I'm okay with it now and I haven't peed on any of the furniture...yet.
